Hawaiian Beach Shrimp

Description

This dish has great and moist shrimps with a great marinade. perfect for summer, celebration, grillings, etc.

Ingredients

Original recipe yields 6 servings

  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon pepper seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons finely minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 pounds extra large shrimp (16-20), peeled and deveined
  • 12 wooden skewers
